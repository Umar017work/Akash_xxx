Step by Step

How to Apply

1

Register for CUET

Visit cuet.samarth.ac.in and register for the Common University Entrance Test (CUET-UG) conducted by NTA.

2

Appear in CUET

Take the CUET examination in your selected subjects. SPMC accepts CUET scores for all undergraduate admissions.

3

Apply on DU Portal

After CUET results, register on the DU Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS) at du.ac.in and fill your preferences.

4

Accept Seat & Pay Fee

Accept the allocated seat within the deadline and pay the admission fee online through the DU portal.

5

Document Verification

Report to SPM College for document verification with originals and photocopies of all required documents.

Financial Support

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit Scholarship

Available to students who secure top ranks in CUET and maintain excellent academic performance throughout the year.

SC/ST/OBC Aid

Post-Matric Scholarship from GoI for students belonging to SC, ST, and OBC categories. Apply via scholarship.gov.in.

EWS / Minority Aid

Financial assistance for economically weaker sections and minority community students as per central and state government schemes.

College Aid Fund

SPMC's own student welfare fund for students facing financial hardship. Apply through the College office.
